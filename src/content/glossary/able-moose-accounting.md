**Able Moose Accounting** is the canonical fictional customer used in lesson examples and quiz prompts across learn.msp.au. The same company appears at three scales so that a learner who completes Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced builds a coherent mental model of how the same kind of customer evolves with size.

- **Beginner — Able Moose Accounting**, a 15-person bookkeeping firm in one office, one Microsoft 365 tenant, default policies, mostly Windows laptops.
- **Intermediate — Able Moose Accounting (mid-market)**, 120 staff across three offices, hybrid working, finance/HR split, multiple Network Sites, baseline + override policy structure.
- **Advanced — Able Moose Group**, 1,800 staff across 14 acquired sub-firms in a parent/child organisation structure, regional compliance variation, in-house IT collaborating with the MSP.

When you see Able Moose in a lesson, you're not meeting a new toy company, you're seeing the persona the learner met at the previous level, scaled.
