**Meeting Branding** extends Exclaimer's signature governance into video calls. A Meeting Branding theme draws on the Icon and Background assets in a Brand Kit, plus its own Nametag content settings (display-name and job-title fonts, colours, transparency).

Each platform implements the theme slightly differently. **Microsoft Teams** users download the theme's icon, nametag overlay, or background from the User Details Editor and apply them as a Teams background; transparent backgrounds work as blurred backgrounds. **Google Meet** doesn't support transparent overlays, so the nametag has to be baked into the background image before upload. **Zoom** integrates an Exclaimer Zoom App with the Zoom Desktop client; overlays show the participant's display name, job title, optional brand icon, and a chosen picture background.
