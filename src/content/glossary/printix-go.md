**Printix Go** is the on-device sign-in and release software that installs on supported multifunction printers (MFPs). Once Printix Go is on the printer, users authenticate at the touchscreen, with a swipe card, an ID code plus PIN, or both, and release their pending documents directly. No phone required.

Printix Go is supported on a wide list of vendors: Brother, Canon, Epson, Fujifilm, HP (including HP Workpath), Konica Minolta, Kyocera, Lexmark, Ricoh, and Xerox. As of 6 July 2022 it ships enabled for all new and existing tenants, so the historical "must apply for Printix Go authorization" gate is gone. Each vendor has its own preparation steps; budget time for that during a rollout.
