**Whitelabel** is what lets an MSP present a vendor product as part of its own service. The vendor provides the platform; the MSP provides the brand. Customers see "filtering by Acme MSP" rather than "filtering by Vendor X."

For DNSFilter that includes a custom dashboard subdomain (e.g. `filtering.yourdomain.com`), brand-replaced alert and notification emails, and per-customer block-page logos. It's a partner-account feature, opt-in at signup, and one of the standard reasons MSPs choose DNSFilter over more direct-to-customer competitors.
