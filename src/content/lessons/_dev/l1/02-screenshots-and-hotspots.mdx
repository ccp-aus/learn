## Notes on shape

Labels are short by design: numeric markers (1, 2, 3) keep the dot uncluttered and let the popover carry the real text. Descriptive content belongs in `title`, `purpose`, and the body. Tones outside `primary | warning | success` render unstyled and are easy to miss in review.
