This is the last lesson in the kitchen-sink course. It checks two things at once: that the inline `Checkpoint` element hydrates and accepts answers, and that the end-of-lesson chrome (LessonProgress, prev/next nav, final-quiz CTA) wires through to the final quiz.

## Inline checkpoint

Checkpoints are tiny mid-lesson quizzes. They use the same renderer as the final quiz but render compactly and do NOT persist scores. Wrong answers explain themselves, then let you try again.

## End-of-lesson wrap-up

When you scroll past this line, you should see:

- The **LessonProgress** card with a "Mark complete" button. Clicking it ticks completion in localStorage and offers a Continue link to the next lesson.
- A **prev / next** navigation row. Previous should link back to `05-mermaid-diagrams`. Next should link forward to `07-annotated-text-artefacts`.
- A breadcrumb up top that points back through course → vendor → home.

If any of those three pieces are missing or pointing at the wrong place, the lesson layout has regressed.
